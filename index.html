Privacy Policy - Postcard Photo

Effective date: March 3, 2026

Postcard Photo respects your privacy.

1) Data We Collect

Postcard Photo does not collect personal data on our servers.

2) Device Permissions and Why They Are Used

  • Camera: to let you capture photos inside the app.
  • Photo Library (Add Only): to save framed images to your library.
  • Location (Optional): only when you turn on Include location, used to add a place label to your photo.

3) Data Storage

  • Image processing is done on-device.
  • App preferences (for example: title options and selected camera) are stored locally on your device.
  • We do not sell or share personal data with third parties.
  • The app does not use advertising SDKs or analytics SDKs.

4) User Control

You can change Camera, Photos, and Location permissions any time in iOS Settings.

5) Retention and Deletion

  • Local app settings remain on your device until you uninstall the app or clear app data.
  • Saved photos remain in your Photos library until you delete them.

6) Children

This app is not directed to children under 13 and does not knowingly collect personal data from children.

7) Contact

For privacy questions, contact: support@thachanhitt.com

8) Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The latest version is always available on this page.
